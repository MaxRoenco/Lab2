About Us

A Space Where Art
Comes to Life

Kvadrat Acting Academy was founded with a single mission: to give every aspiring actor in Moldova the tools, confidence, and technique to tell powerful stories on any stage.

Our instructors are professional theatre and film practitioners who bring real-world experience into every class. Whether you're stepping on stage for the first time or preparing for a professional audition, Kvadrat has a program for you.
